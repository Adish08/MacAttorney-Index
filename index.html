About This Project

One of the great strengths of the Macintosh is the excellent software that comes included right out of the box, adding significant value. Apple's standard applications are impressively competent, handling many common tasks effectively. However, they don't always cover every niche or provide the exact features you might need. Plus, some types of essential software just aren't part of the package.

While there's a vast world of commercial software available for your Mac, investing in new applications isn't always feasible. The good news? There's also a fantastic array of free software available that can fill the gaps and expand what you can do with your Mac. Many of these free tools are surprisingly robust and well worth adding to your toolkit.

Here, we've compiled a list of free Mac applications that we consider particularly valuable or highly useful. We hope this curated selection helps you discover some great new software, whether you're new to the Mac or have been using one for years.

How We Select Software

  • Must be genuinely free (no trialware or freemium)
  • Must be actively maintained
  • Must have a good reputation in the community
  • Must provide clear value to users
  • Must be compatible with recent macOS versions
